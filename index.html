Welcome!

I am a Research and Instrument Analyst at the Space Telescope Science Institute since July 2012; my job is to provide support for Instrument Scientists and the Hubble Space Telescope (HST) General Observers in the processing and analysis of scientific, calibration and engineering data I am currently assigned to the Near Infrared Spectrograph (NIRSpec) for the upcoming James Webb Space Telescope (JWST), for which I will provide support as well once launched.


The Space Telescope Science Institute operates the science program for the Hubble Space Telescope and will conduct the science and missions operations for the James Webb Space Telescope and supports other astronomy programs and conduct world-class scientific research. The Space Telescope Science Institute is operated by the Association of Universities for Research in Astronomy (AURA) for NASA.
